# gesture-tool
A tool which sits on your desk and do cool thing

# Story
Hi everyone! So, as we know the world is ending and what if your keyboard doesn't work but your hands does and you have rick roll playing? It would be like FAHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH. How are you gonna decrease the volume or skip the song. That's when our project comes in!! So with this thing you can just do gestures in the air and things happens( things = commands ) so now you can just turn the volume up, volume down OR SKIP THE RICK AND ROLL.... And when you move your finger anti-clockwise it just goes 676767676767676767676767
